Unlock Afrika

In return, we share the success, with profits split 40% to us and 60% to the business. Our goal is to fuel innovation and support the next generation of African businesses, helping them reach new heights.

Unlock Afrika App: Brief Overview

Unlock Afrika is a versatile mobile app that offers users an integrated experience for logistics services, shopping, and culinary training. With over 20,000 downloads, the app is designed to cater to the diverse needs of users across Africa and beyond.
